Pesto Pasta

Description

Easy pasta dish ready in under 10 minutes

Add chicken or salmon for extra munch

Serve with rocket leaves and sundried tomatoes for added colour!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or Linguine - I use wholemeal for extra fibre
  • Frozen peas, or green veg of choice
  • Frozen spinach
  • Homemade pesto or jar of shop-bought pesto
  • optional parmesan cheese or basil leaves to garnish

Recipe

  1. Put a large pan of salted water on to boil
  2. Add pasta - quantity according to number of servings required
  3. Add a handful of peas and some spinach
  4. Cook for about 9 to 11 minutes depending if you like your pasta al dente
  5. Drain and add a good dollop of pesto. Stir in and serve
