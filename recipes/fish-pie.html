Fish Pie

Description

A real crowd-pleaser! Swap in any fish you like in this versatile, comforting pie.

Serves 4

1 hour 10 minutes to cook

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 sticks celery
  • 150g cheddar cheese
  • 1 lemon
  • 1/2 fresh red chilli
  • 4 sprigs of fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 300g salmon fillets
  • 300g unsmoked haddock, cod or white fish
  • 125 g king prawns, optional
  • olive oil
  • 1 good handful baby spinach
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, optional

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 200C/400F/gas 6
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut into 2cm chunks, then cook in boiling salted water for 12 minutes, or until tender
  3. Peel and grate the carrot into a deep baking dish, then coarsely grate the celery and cheddar
  4. Finely grate the lemon zest and chilli. Add to the dish with the parsely
  5. Slice the fish into bite-size chuncks and add to the tray with the prawns, if using
  6. Squeeze over the lemon juice - no pips! - drizzle lightly with oil and add a good pinch of salt and pepper
  7. Add spinach and tomatoes if using then mix everything really well
  8. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pan. Mash with a couple of glugs of olive oil. Season
  9. Spread the mash over the fish pie mix and bake in the oven fpr 40 minutes until cooked through and golden on top
  10. Serve with steamed green veg or salad and ketchup for the kids
